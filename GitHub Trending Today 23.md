No.1

![](https://githubawesome.com/content/images/2026/02/image-157.png)

Local development with port numbers sucks. Port conflicts, forgotten ports, wrong apps on cached tabs. Monorepos make it worse. AI agents guess wrong ports. Localhost cookies bleed across apps. Browser history is chaotic. Vercel Labs built Portless to fix this. One line in package.json: portless myapp next dev. Now your app is at myapp.localhost:1355. Stable URLs, no conflicts.

[https://github.com/vercel-labs/portless](https://github.com/vercel-labs/portless?ref=githubawesome.com)
